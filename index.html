👋 Welcome to GoodSerialPlotter

Plot live serial data straight in your browser — no install, no backend. It talks to your device over the Web Serial API.

Get started in 4 steps

  1. Pick a baud rate matching your device.
  2. Click Connect and choose the serial port in the browser prompt.
    No device? Tick Dev mode first to try a fake 1 kHz signal.
  3. Set the Delimiter to match your output (comma, tab, space, …).
  4. Click Traces ▾ on a plot and tick the channels to show.

Data formats (auto-detected)

  • Plain: 1.2,3.4,5.6
  • Labeled: temp:25.1,rpm:1200 — series get named automatically.

Plots

  • Drag the title bar to move; drag the corner to resize.
  • ＋ Add plot for more windows; each shows its own traces.
  • Drag on a chart to zoom; Reset zoom resumes live view.
  • The x-axis is fixed (T+ relative to now); data scrolls under it.
  • Σ toggles per-trace stats (min / max / avg / std / count).

More

  • Serial Monitor tab — raw lines with timestamps, filter, save.
  • Record → Export CSV to capture a session.
  • Reset data clears data but keeps your layout.
  • Save / Load layout stores plots, traces & settings to a file.
  • Your setup is auto-saved and restored on reload.

⚠️ Web Serial needs Chrome, Edge, or Opera over HTTPS (or localhost). Firefox/Safari can still use Dev mode.
